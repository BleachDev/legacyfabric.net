The home of legacy version support for fabric

Legacy fabric is a project based on the Fabric Project. Its two main priorities are to keep parity with upstream and to support as many legacy versions as possible.

We currently Support versions: 1.12.2, 1.8.9, 1.7.10 & 1.6.4
